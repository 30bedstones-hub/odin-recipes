Irish coffee

Description

Warm cocktail that combines coffee, whiskey, sugar, and lightly whipped cream.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 cup of strong hot coffee
  • 1-1.5 oz Irish whiskey
  • 1-2 teaspoons of brown sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons of heavy cream

Steps

  1. Fill a heatproof glass or mug with hot coffee.
  2. Stir in the sugar until dissolved.
  3. Add the whiskey and mix.
  4. Lightly whip the cream (it should still be pourable, not stiff).
  5. Gently pour the cream over the back of a spoon so it floats on top.
  6. Enjoy!
