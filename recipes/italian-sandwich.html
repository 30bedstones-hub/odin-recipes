Italian sandwich

"Prosciutto di Parma, Tagliere, Italia" by Luca Dea, via Wikimedia Commons, licensed under CC BY 4.0.

Description

A light Italian-style sandwich with simple, high-quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 small ciabatta or focaccia roll
  • 2-3 slices of prosciutto
  • 1 slice of fresh mozzarella
  • A handful of arugula
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Slice the bread and lightly toast it if you want extra texture.
  2. Drizzle a little olive oil inside.
  3. Layer prosciutto, mozzarella, and arugula.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. (Optional) Add a few drops of balsamic vinegar for sweetness and acidity.
  6. Close and press lightly.
  7. Enjoy!
