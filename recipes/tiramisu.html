Tiramisu

"Tiramisu" by Raffaele Diomede, via Wikimedia Commons, licensed under CC BY 2.0.

Description

Italian dessert made of layers of biscuits soaked in coffee and cream.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 2-3 savoiardi
  • 2-3 tablespoons of cooled espresso
  • 1/4 cup of mascarpone cheese
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 small egg
  • A pinch of vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of cocoa powder
  • 1-2 teaspoons of coffee liqueur (optional)

Steps

  1. Brew espresso and let it cool.
  2. Whisk egg yolk and sugar until pale and creamy.
  3. Mix in mascarpone until smooth.
  4. (Optional) Fold in whipped egg white or whipped cream for a lighter texture.
  5. Quickly dip savoiardi in coffee (don't soak too long).
  6. Layer: savoiardi -> cream -> savoiardi -> cream.
  7. Dust with cocoa powder.
  8. Chill for at least 2-4 hours (overnight is even better).
  9. Enjoy!
